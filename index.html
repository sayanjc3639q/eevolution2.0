About EEvolution

EEvolution is more than just a website; it's a digital ecosystem built by students, for students. Our mission is to centralize academic resources, streamline communication, and foster a collaborative environment for the Electrical Engineering department.

Whether you're looking for last-minute exam notes, checking the latest schedule, or reliving campus memories, EEvolution is your one-stop destination.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common queries about the platform.

How do I contribute to EEvolution?

You can verify your student ID and start uploading notes directly. For code contributions, check our GitHub repository linked in the footer!

Is this platform open to other departments?

Currently, EEvolution is tailored for the Electrical Engineering department, but we plan to expand to other branches soon.

How are Evo Coins calculated?

Evo Coins are awarded based on the quality and frequency of your contributions. Verified notes earn more than general uploads.
